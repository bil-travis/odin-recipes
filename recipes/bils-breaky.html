Bil's Favourite Breakfast

Description

You all have no idea who I (Bil) am, but this is my go to breakfast each morning. I break it up into two parts, a simple omelette and a protein shake. The prep time is minimal and it feeds one person.

Ingredients

  • Omelette
    • 4 Eggs
    • Water
    • Mushroom
    • Green Pepper
    • Green Onion
    • Salsa and Jalapeno Aioli
  • Protein Shake
    • 2 Scoops of Your Protein of Choice (Optimum Nutrition Double chocolate for myself)
    • 10 mg of Monohydrate Creatine (Optional)
    • 1 Frozen Banana
    • 0.5 Cup Frozen Strawberries
    • 2 Tbsp of Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Omelette:
    1. Dice all vegetables and throw into the pan on low.
    2. While the vegetables simmer crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk in water.
    3. Let cook until eggs show air small air bubbles, then flip in half.
    4. Top with salsa and aioli as required.
  2. Protein shake:
    1. Add cold water to blender.
    2. Mix in protein powder, creatine frozen fruit and peanut butter.
    3. Blend until fluid.
