The Best Lasagna You'll Ever Make

Description

This won't be easy but trust me it will be worth it. This lasagna is a prefect balance of creamy and cheesy. So grab your favourite apron and pre heat your oven to 350F for us to get started.

Be ready for a 30 minute prep time and a 2 hour and 30 minute cook time (total time: 3 hours and 15 minutes). Overall this will serve 12 people (or less if your guests are as hungry as I am).

Ingredients

  • Extra Lean Ground Beer
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Chopped White Onion
  • Two cloves of Garlic
  • Canned Tomato Products
    • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
    • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
    • 2 Cans Tomato Paste
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Spices and Seasoning
    • Basil Leaves
    • Fresh Parsley
    • Salt
    • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna as follows:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat and top with Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and slap it into the oven.
  6. Let rest before serving.
